DIGITAL HEROES

A SUPERHERO THEMED NIGHT OUT
6TH JUNE 2015. NQ, MANCHESTER.

Well, well, well. It's that time again, already! And this time, it's a big one! By now you probably know the drill, but for the newbies out there, the 4th Digital Heroes is here!

As per tradition on my birthday; a fancy dress night out with all my closest friends & wonderful Web/digital folk of Manchester. (Don't worry, fancy dress is optional!), but a lot of us definitely go all out, so feel free to get involved! - see previous years below for ideas.

This year we'll also be celebrating Jag's send off to fatherhood - also known as the death of Jag. (RIP, Jag.)

The plan is as follows:

18:00, SAT 6TH @ HOLD FAST BAR, NQ

We've got the awesome cinema room at Hold Fast Bar, NQ (50 Newton Street, Manchester M1 2EA) booked all evening! It's got its own A/V system, and a huge projector. So the plan is to watch the Champions League Final, play Mario Kart / Smash Bros / Splatoon for the Wii U, and listen to beltin' tunes; then potentially head over the road to the Crown & Anchor for a game of pool, then off to Satan's Hollow.
